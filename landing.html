As Seattle has grown into one of the most expensive cities in the country, the burden of rising housing costs has not fallen equally. This dashboard maps where Mandatory Housing Affordability zoning policies apply and tracks how rent burden — paying more than 50% of income on housing — has shifted across racial and income groups from 2006 to 2022.
